  • The old Beijing sesame sauce hot pot is a representative of the northern hot pot, which is characterized by its light soup bottom and rich seasonings, especially preferring to use sesame sauce as the main seasoning. Hot pot ingredients are mainly meat, especially mutton and beef, maintaining the traditional flavor of northern hot pot. The mellow aroma of sesame sauce and hot pot ingredients bring a unique taste experience for diners.
  • The old Beijing sesame sauce hot pot is loved by diners, and its unique sesame sauce flavor is memorable.
