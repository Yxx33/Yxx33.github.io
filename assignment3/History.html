The culture of the ancient capital of Beijing mainly refers to the cultural structure, cultural elements and ethos created by Beijing as the central region of the country and the center of foreign exchanges and exchanges in the Liao, Jin, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties, representing the characteristics of Chinese culture and the highest level of cultural development at that time.

The culture of the ancient capital of Beijing is rich, and the culture of the imperial capital of the five dynasties as the carrier is its main body. The material and cultural aspects of cities, palaces, temples, temples, canals and roads, as well as clothes and hairstyles, food, boats, and so on, as well as spiritual and cultural ideas, laws and regulations, language, literature and art, folk customs and so on, are all dazzling, all with the weather of the emperor, which is vast and exquisite.
